Who We Are

MORE THAN JUST A GYM

EVOX FIT is a premium fitness concept in the heart of Newark, NJ — built for those who are serious about results. With custom weightlifting machines, a dedicated crosstraining area, and a team that truly cares, we're redefining what a local gym can be.

  • Spotless, well-maintained facilities — always.
  • Unique, custom-built equipment you won't find elsewhere.
  • Dedicated staff committed to your progress.
  • Free parking — no excuses not to show up.
